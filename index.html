Google Drive backup disclosure

If you choose to enable backup, CivilCalc Pro uses Google Sign-In and the Google Drive drive.appdata permission to back up your app data to your own Google account in the hidden appDataFolder. This access is used only for backup and restore.

What the app does

CivilCalc Pro helps users estimate materials, quantities, and approximate costs for common construction workflows. It also lets users save projects and previous calculations locally on their device.

  • Concrete, steel, masonry, plaster, paint, tile, and related calculators
  • Saved projects, saved calculations, and favorites
  • PDF and CSV export and operating-system share features

Optional account features

Some features require internet access or third-party services.

  • Google Drive backup and restore through your Google account
  • Google Play billing for subscriptions and one-time purchases
  • Google Mobile Ads for the free tier and rewarded access flows

Important disclaimer

CivilCalc Pro is an estimation and workflow tool. It is not a substitute for licensed engineering advice, code compliance review, structural design approval, surveying, or site-specific professional judgment.

Always verify outputs against local codes, project drawings, site conditions, manufacturer guidance, lab tests, and qualified professionals before procurement or construction decisions.
