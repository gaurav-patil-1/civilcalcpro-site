Features

Everything a site engineer actually uses

Designed for quick estimation on the job: focused calculators, saved projects, exports your team can read, and optional cloud backup when you want it.

Quantity calculators

Concrete volume, slabs, beams, columns, footings, tanks, brick and block walls, plaster, paint, tiles, excavation and formwork - with material breakdowns for cement, sand, aggregate, and steel.

Quick RCC checks

Slab, beam, column and footing quick-design helpers for common layouts. Useful on-site for sanity checks before referring back to drawings, codes, or qualified review.

Unit converter

Length, area, volume, weight, pressure, speed, time and fuel - with sensible defaults for metric and imperial, and clean number formatting that matches the rest of the app.

Projects, history & favorites

Group calculations into projects, pin frequent calculators, save reusable defaults, and reopen anything from a clean history view. No accounts, no logins - just your work.

Clean PDF & CSV export

Share a polished PDF report with your team or client, or pipe the numbers into a spreadsheet via CSV. Export uses the standard device share sheet - no third party in between.

Privacy you can trust

Project data stays on your device by default. Google Drive backup uses the hidden drive.appdata folder in your account - we never see your project content, and we don’t sell it.

Your data, your device

Local-first by design

Most apps in this category send everything to a server. CivilCalc Pro doesn’t. Projects, calculations, favorites, and settings live on your phone unless you explicitly enable backup.

  • No CivilCalc Pro account. No sign-up form, no password reset emails.
  • No project content sold. We don’t sell your project data or backup content for money.
  • Works offline. Calculators run on-device - useful when you’re on a site with weak signal.
Optional cloud backup

Backup to your own Google Drive

If you turn it on, backup uses the drive.appdata scope to write to a hidden CivilCalc Pro folder inside your Google account. Other apps can’t see it, and you can disconnect or delete it any time.

  • App-specific scope. Access is limited to CivilCalc Pro’s own backup files - not your wider Drive.
  • One-tap restore. Reinstalling? Sign in and pull your latest backup back down.
  • Always reversible. Disconnect the account or delete the cloud backup from inside the app.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to create an account?

No. CivilCalc Pro doesn’t have its own account system. Your projects and calculations are saved on your device. Google sign-in is only used if you choose to enable backup.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. All calculators run on-device. Internet is only needed for optional features - Drive backup, Google Play purchases, and ads in the free tier.

What does the free tier include?

Most calculators are available in the free tier with occasional interstitial ads, plus rewarded-ad unlocks for select advanced tools. A paid upgrade removes ads and unlocks the full set without the rewarded gate.

Where are my projects stored?

On your device. If you enable Google Drive backup, an encrypted-at-rest copy is also written to the hidden drive.appdata folder inside your own Google account.

Can I export results to share with my team?

Yes. Use PDF for a clean report or CSV for spreadsheets. Exports go through the standard Android share sheet, so you stay in control of who receives them.

Is CivilCalc Pro a substitute for an engineer?

No. It’s an estimation and workflow tool. Always verify outputs against project drawings, local codes, manufacturer guidance, and qualified professional review before procurement or construction.
